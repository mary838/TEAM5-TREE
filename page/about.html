What is our Grow ?

Choose the Right Tree :

Select a tree species that is suitable for your climate, soil, and space.


Planting Location :

Find a spot with enough sunlight, proper soil, and space for the tree to grow .


Dig a Hole :

Dig a hole about twice the size of the tree’s root ball. The hole should be deep enough so the top of the root ball


Prepare the Tree :

Remove the tree from its container, gently shake off excess soil, and loosen any tangled roots.


Plant the Tree :

Place the tree in the hole, making sure it is standing upright. Fill in the hole with soil, pressing it down lightly as you go to remove air pockets.


Watering:

Water the tree thoroughly after planting, and keep the soil moist (but not soaked) for the first few months to help the roots establish.


Mulching:

Apply mulch around the base of the tree to help retain moisture, suppress weeds, and regulate soil temperature.


Ongoing Care:

Water regularly, prune when needed, and protect the tree from pests or diseases.
